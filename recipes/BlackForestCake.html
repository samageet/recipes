------------------Black Forest Cake------------------


A rich chocolate layer cake topped with sweet cherries delivering timeless indulgence.

Ingredients

  • About 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1¾ cups plain (all‑purpose) flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup neutral‑flavoured oil (e.g. vegetable)
  • 1 cup cooled, strong brewed coffee
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • One 21‑oz tin of cherry pie filling
  • ½ cup cherry‑flavoured liqueur

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 °C (350 °F) and grease two 20 cm (8 in) cake tins, then dust them with flour.
  2. Combine the milk and vinegar in a small jug; set aside to curdle.
  3. Sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt into a bowl.
  4. In a separate large bowl, whisk the eggs, oil, cold brewed coffee and vanilla extract, then stir in the curdled milk.
  5. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture, mixing until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared tins and bake for about 30 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the centre emerges clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool fully in their tins before transferring to a wire rack.
  8. For the filling, stir together the cherry pie filling and liqueur, then chill until ready to assemble.
