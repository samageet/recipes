--------------Grilled Cheese Sandwich--------------


A warm, melty grilled cheese sandwich that brings you back home.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough or white bread
  • 1 slice sharp cheddar
  • 1 slice mozzarella
  • 1 slice smoked gouda or provolone (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 clove garlic (cut in half)
  • 1-2 slices of tomato (optional)
  • Pinch of dried oregano or mixed Italian herbs
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Lightly rub the garlic clove on one side of each bread slice.
  3. Spread mayonnaise on the inside of one slice. Layer the cheeses and tomato slices, then sprinkle a pinch of herbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Close the sandwich. Butter the outsides generously.
  5. Place in the skillet. Press down lightly. Cook until golden and crisp, 2-3 minutes per side. Flip carefully.
  6. Once both sides are golden and the cheese is melted, remove from the pan. Let rest for 1 minute, then slice and serve hot.
