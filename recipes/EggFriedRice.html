--------------------Egg Fried Rice--------------------


A savoury stir-fry of rice and eggs, ideal for a quick weekday supper or a late-night comfort meal..

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of quick-cook rice
  • 1 teaspoon of cooking oil
  • ½ finely chopped onion
  • ½ cup of peas or chopped green beans
  • 1 large egg, whisked
  • ¼ teaspoon of black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Stir in the uncooked instant rice, cover, and remove from heat. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Sauté chopped onion and green beans until tender.
  5. Push veggies aside and pour in the beaten egg; scramble until just cooked.
  6. Add the softened instant rice, fluffing it into the mixture.
  7. Season with black pepper and stir until everything is well combined and heated through.
