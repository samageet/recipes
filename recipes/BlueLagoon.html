---------------Blue Lagoon Margarita---------------


A cool summer drink that looks good and goes down easy.

Ingredients

  • Approximately 1 cup of ice (add more if you like it extra chilled)
  • 2 fl oz of sour mix
  • 1½ fl oz of pineapple juice
  • 1¼ fl oz of tequila
  • 1 fl oz of blue curaçao
  • ½ fl oz of freshly squeezed lime juice

Steps

  1. Fill a blender with ice.
  2. Add the sour mix, pineapple juice, tequila, blue curaçao, and lime juice.
  3. Secure the lid and blend until the drink is completely smooth.
  4. Serve immediately in chilled glasses.
